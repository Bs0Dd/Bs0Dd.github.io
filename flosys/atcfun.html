Hi,
Had anyone noticed that the "AT+CFUN=0", once processed through NCDS, is
actually simulating keyboard input?
I was capturing dumps and trying to implement a "power-off" function...
Using a normal terminal I was issuing "AT+CFUN=0". Didn't think too much
about it when it wasn't working and the message to unlock the keyboard
appeared on my phone. Once unlocked, it worked fine.
Each time, NCDS sends these two frames:
        1E 00 0C 0C 00 08 00 01 00 42 01 0D 01 42 12 08
        1E 00 0C 0C 00 08 00 01 00 42 02 0D 01 40 11 0A

When I tried implementing in gnokii (I'm only half-way there), I realized
the the first frame appears to be the same as pressing the power button,
and the second pressing the 'Ok' button.

Has anyone seen this behaviour also?

automatically change the current profile at a given time... :-) )
